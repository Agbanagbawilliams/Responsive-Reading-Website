Charlotte's Web
by E.B. White

About:

It tells the story of a livestock pig named Wilbur and his friendship with a barn spider named Charlotte. When he is in danger of being slaughtered, Charlotte writes messages in her web praising Wilbur, such as "Some Pig", "Terrific", "Radiant", and "Humble", to persuade the farmer, Homer Zuckerman, to let him live.
