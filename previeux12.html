Dog Man Unleashed
by Dav Pilkey

About:

Having the head of a dog and the body of a human isn't as easy as it looks. ​​​​​​​When Greg the police dog and his cop companion are injured on the job, a life-saving surgery changes the course of history, and Dog Man is born. This heroic hound digs into deception, claws after crooks, and rolls over robbers.
