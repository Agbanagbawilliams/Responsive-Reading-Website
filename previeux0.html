The One And Only Ivan
by Katherine Applegate

About:

The One and Only Ivan is a heartwarming and moving story inspired by the real-life tale of Ivan, a gorilla who lived in captivity for 27 years in a shopping mall. The novel is narrated by Ivan himself, offering a unique and deeply emotional perspective. Ivan is a silverback gorilla who lives in the Exit 8 Big Top Mall and Video Arcade. His home is a glass-enclosed domain, and he spends his days watching TV, drawing pictures, and observing humans. Despite his circumstances, Ivan has grown accustomed to captivity and doesn't often think about his life in the wild. Everything changes when Ruby, a baby elephant, arrives at the mall. Ruby's arrival prompts Ivan to see their captivity through fresh eyes. Inspired by Stella's wish for Ruby to live a better life, Ivan begins to use his artistic talents to draw attention to their plight and find a way to save her.
