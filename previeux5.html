Holes
by Louis Sachar

About:

The story follows Stanley Yelnats, a boy who is sent to a juvenile detention center called Camp Green Lake for a crime he didn't commit. At the camp, boys are forced to dig large holes in the desert every day as part of their "character building." As Stanley digs, he begins to uncover the truth about the camp's real purpose and its connection to his family's curse, a treasure, and a famous outlaw, Kissing Kate Barlow.
